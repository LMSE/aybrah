SGD Description
Core component of transport protein particle (TRAPP) complexes I-III; TRAPP complexes are related multimeric guanine nucleotide-exchange factors for the GTPase Ypt1p, regulating ER-Golgi traffic (TRAPPI), intra-Golgi traffic (TRAPPII), endosome-Golgi traffic (TRAPPII and III) and autophagy (TRAPPIII); mutation of conserved residues leads to defects in endocytic recycling and a block in sporulation/meiosis; mutations in the human homolog cause the spondyloepiphyseal dysplasia
