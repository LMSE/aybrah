SGD Description
Seipin involved in lipid droplet morphology, number, and size; directly promotes initiation of lipid droplet formation on the ER, and ensures that droplets bud from the ER towards the cytosolic side of the membrane; related to the human BSCL2 which is associated with lipodystrophy
