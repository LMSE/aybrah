SGD Description
Protein involved in control of cell wall structure and stress response; direct inhibitor of the nuclear Dbf2 related (NDR) kinase Cbk1p-Mob2p; overproduction confers resistance to cell-wall degrading enzymes; exhibits genetic interactions with genes involved in the cell wall integrity pathway; LRE1 has a paralog, HLR1, that arose from the whole genome duplication
